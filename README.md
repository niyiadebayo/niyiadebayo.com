# niyiadebayo.com

Static personal site for Adeniyi Adebayo. The writing direction is a thesis library, not a
chronological blog: operator essays on emerging-market cities, marketplaces, informal systems,
distribution, and capital formation, with a short personal arc that explains the operator behind the
work.

## Editorial Direction

The core thesis: capital compounds city by city, through necessity rails, informal trust systems,
distribution density, and data that makes assets financeable.

New essays should deepen one of four lanes:

- How city operating density is measured beyond country GDP, population, and TAM.
- How local operators compound through transport companies, agents, merchants, and pharmacists.
- How capital enters informal systems by making assets, people, and fleets underwritable.
- How secondary cities become platforms before capital crowds into the obvious capitals.

The main homepage arc is the reading path. Avoid adding short note-like pieces to it unless they are
expanded into full essays or deliberately moved outside the main arc.

## Quality Bar

- One clear mechanism per essay.
- Ground-level city examples in the opening.
- Numbers in prose, with footnotes at the end.
- Cross-link repeated concepts to their primary essay instead of restating the full argument.
- Keep the voice terse, specific, and operator-led; follow `AGENTS.md` before editing.
